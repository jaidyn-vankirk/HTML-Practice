Recent Defeat

A few days ago, the Cat Burglers got away again. After robbing the Chase bank, the cat bandits went to make an escape when they came face to face with Captain Jaidyn in an alleyway. Before Jaidyn could use any of her special moves, Dobby Hypnotized her with his cloudy eye of mystery, which gave Shady times to use his claws of terror. These moves allowed them to make their great escape. Beware, they could still be in the Sherman area.

Tensions Rising

Captain Jaidyn and the Cat Burglars have not crossed paths recently, but that does not mean Shady and Dobby are not causing mischief. After breaking into the Austin College Adam's Center, they visited students with cat allergies. Two people were hospitalized with valuables taken, and five more suffered allergic reactions.
